The Journey

HOW WE
GOT HERE

💡
2022

THE IDEA SPARKS

Founders Marcus Holt and Kiri Nakamura — an AI researcher and a business educator — watched their networks struggle to keep up with the AI wave. Most courses were either too technical or too shallow. They decided to build the one they wished existed.

🛠️
Early 2023

BUILDING THE CURRICULUM

Over six months, the team interviewed 200+ hiring managers, business owners, and employees to understand exactly what AI skills the world actually needed. Every module was designed from real-world need, not academic theory.

🚀
September 2023

LAUNCH DAY

AI Nexus Academy went live. Within 72 hours, 800 students had enrolled. The feedback validated everything — this was exactly what the market needed. We hit 2,000 students within the first month.

🌏
2024

GOING GLOBAL

Students from 47 countries enrolled. Enterprise partnerships began with firms in finance, healthcare, and tech. The course curriculum expanded to include Module 7's deep-dive into generative AI tools as the landscape evolved.

🏆
2025

12,000+ CERTIFIED

Over twelve thousand professionals have now earned their AI Optimization Certificate. Student outcomes continue to exceed expectations — with documented salary increases, business growth, and career transitions across every industry.
